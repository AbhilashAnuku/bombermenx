Faction-themed bombs · Vāyu vs Agni

Each side of the arena fields bombs in its own elemental palette. JavaFX paints them via colour and particle systems — no extra dependencies, deterministic, server-authoritative.

Vāyu · water
Cool palette
Body #0fb5a9, fuse #3aedff, blast #9ee8ff. Splash particles, slow fade.
Agni · fire
Warm palette
Body #ff9933, fuse #ffd700, blast #ff5a1f. Ember particles, sharp falloff.
Neutral · shared
Pickup-thrown
Body #ffd700, fuse #e6eef8, blast white. Used for bombs from neutral pickups (Super Bomb, Kick).
Implementation
JavaFX, not R3F
The desktop client is JavaFX (Java). R3F runs in JS/React and would only fit the web-viewer side. Faction colour comes from GameConfig.factionPalette(side); particles from ArenaRenderer.spawnBlast(side, cell).
Rule
Colour is signal
Players read whose bomb is on the grid before the timer starts. Determinism rules: colour is cosmetic; behaviour (radius, fuse, chain) is identical across factions.
JavaFX Color.web() palette · particles via javafx.scene.canvas.GraphicsContext · zero new deps.
